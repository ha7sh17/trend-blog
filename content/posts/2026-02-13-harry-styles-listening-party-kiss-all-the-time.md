Harry Styles just sent his massive fanbase into a frenzy. The British pop superstar announced exclusive **Harry Styles listening parties** in 40 cities around the globe, giving lucky fans the chance to hear his highly anticipated fourth solo album before it officially drops. If you've been counting down the days until *Kiss All the Time. Disco, Occasionally* hits streaming platforms, this might be your golden ticket.

## What Are the Harry Styles Listening Parties?

On Thursday, February 12, 2026, Harry Styles took to Instagram to reveal something special for his devoted Harries. The pre-release listening parties will let selected fans experience the full album together, weeks before its official March 6 release date.

"We wanna dance with all our friends," Styles wrote in his Instagram caption, accompanied by a promotional poster showing the singer in a black sweater with a smiley-face cutout. The events promise one thing above all else: attendees will get to "hear the album in full."

The listening parties kick off on **February 18, 2026**, giving superfans nearly three weeks of bragging rights over everyone else waiting for the album drop. It's a bold move that builds incredible anticipation while rewarding his most dedicated supporters.

## How to Sign Up for Harry Styles Kiss All The Time Listening Parties

Getting your spot at one of these exclusive events requires quick action and a bit of luck. Here's exactly how to apply:

1. **Visit the official page**: Head to the listening party section on Harry's We Belong Together website at webelongtogether.co/listeningparties
2. **Wait in the queue**: Like any high-demand Harry Styles event, expect digital lines
3. **Enter your details**: Provide your name, email address, date of birth, and country
4. **Answer the creative question**: You'll be asked "What do you think listening to the album will feel like?"
5. **Cross your fingers**: Successful applicants receive invitations on **February 16, 2026**

That last question is particularly interesting. Styles has always valued his connection with fans, and asking for their emotional expectations before they've heard a single track from the album shows he genuinely cares about their experience.

## Kiss All The Time. Disco, Occasionally: What We Know About Harry's New Album

This fourth solo studio album from the former One Direction member has been generating buzz since its announcement. The lead single "Aperture" already topped the Billboard Hot 100, proving Styles hasn't lost his commercial magic during his time away from touring.

The album title itself—*Kiss All the Time. Disco, Occasionally*—hints at a playful, dance-oriented direction. Combined with the listening party tagline about wanting to "dance with all our friends," fans are expecting an album built for movement and joy.

The March 6 release will be followed by something truly special: a one-night-only concert in Manchester, England at Co-op Live. There, Harry will perform the complete new album live for the first time in front of a crowd.

## Together, Together Tour: What Comes After the Album

Once the album officially drops, Harry Styles isn't slowing down. The **Together, Together Tour** launches May 16, 2026 in Amsterdam, taking him around the world throughout the year.

The tour's crown jewel? A staggering **30-night residency at Madison Square Garden** in New York City. This run is already breaking records and setting up to be one of the most talked-about concert residencies in recent memory.

European and international dates span across multiple continents, with Styles set to perform at some of the world's most iconic venues. The tour name "Together, Together" reinforces the communal experience Harry seems to be prioritizing with this entire album cycle.
