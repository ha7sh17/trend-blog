## How the NBA technical-foul suspension rule works

For casual fans who only hear this once a season, here’s the practical framework:

- A player who reaches **16 technical fouls** in the regular season faces a **one-game suspension**.
- If a technical is rescinded after league review, that suspension can be avoided.
- After that point, additional penalties escalate (fines and further suspensions after every two additional technicals).

This matters because Dončić has already been in this zone before. He previously hit the edge of suspension territory this season, and at least one technical in that process was rescinded. So people aren’t just searching “what happened”—they’re searching because they know precedent exists.
