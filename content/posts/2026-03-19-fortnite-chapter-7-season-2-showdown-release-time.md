## What Players Should Do During Season Launch Downtime

You can’t force servers online faster, but you can avoid the most common mistakes:

### 1) Don’t reinstall immediately
A huge number of players waste time reinstalling Fortnite when the issue is server-side. Unless there’s a local file corruption warning, wait.

### 2) Check official channels before troubleshooting hardware
Look at Epic/Fortnite status messaging first. If everyone is affected, router resets won’t solve it.

### 3) Update early, not at peak
If a client patch is available, install it right away. Waiting until prime-time creates longer queue pain.

### 4) Expect staggered stability
“Servers are back” rarely means fully smooth. Authentication may recover first, then matchmaking, then party/lobby consistency.

### 5) Be patient with social rumor noise
At launch, false “it’s fixed” and “it’s dead all day” posts spread at the same time. Trust patterns, not one-off screenshots.
