## What Happened in the Kings vs. Jazz Game

According to ESPN’s recap, Sacramento beat Utah **116-111** at Golden 1 Center. DeRozan carried the offense with a season-high 41 points and 11 assists, while adding efficient shot-making and late-game free throws to close things out.

A few game context details matter:

- Sacramento improved to **18-51** and had won four of its last five.
- Utah fell to **20-48**.
- Both teams were short-handed and deep into difficult seasons.
- Even with those circumstances, DeRozan’s production stood out as elite individual shot creation.

This wasn’t “empty” volume scoring. It was high-usage creation plus playmaking, which is exactly why the 40/10 benchmark still means something in modern NBA pace-and-space basketball.

## The Record Everyone Is Talking About

Multiple trend-linked reports highlighted the same stat:

The age angle is crucial to why this took off. Fans are used to DeRozan as a reliable mid-range scorer, but this game reframed him in a different way: not just a veteran surviving late career, but a veteran still capable of **historic offensive nights**.
