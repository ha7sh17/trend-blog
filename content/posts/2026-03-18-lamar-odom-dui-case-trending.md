## What a Not Guilty Plea Actually Means Here

When celebrity legal stories trend, there is often confusion around what a plea means. In practical terms, a not guilty plea does **not** mean the case is over, and it does **not** mean guilt was established. It means the defendant is requiring the prosecution to prove the case in court.

From this point, the legal process can include:

- evidence review (discovery),
- motions challenging procedures or admissibility,
- potential negotiations,
- and trial scheduling if no plea deal resolves it earlier.

In this case, the next public checkpoint appears to be a July hearing date.
