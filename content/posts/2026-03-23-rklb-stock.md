The most concrete catalyst in circulation is Rocket Lab’s hypersonic test work.

- the contract value is **$190 million**,
- it is described as Rocket Lab’s **largest launch contract** to date,

Why does that matter beyond one number?

If Rocket Lab continues to execute cleanly, the market sees a path to more repeat work and stronger government credibility.

The same report also emphasizes the role of **HASTE** (Hypersonic Accelerator Suborbital Test Electron), a suborbital platform designed to support test conditions that are hard to reproduce in ground facilities. For investors, that speaks to product differentiation: this is not simply “another launch slot,” but a specialized service in a demand area tied to defense priorities.
