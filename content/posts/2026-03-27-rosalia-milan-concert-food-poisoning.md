## What happened at the Milan concert?

The concert took place at **Unipol Forum in Milan** on March 25. Reports indicate the show was cut short about an hour in.

By all accounts, that’s exactly what happened: she attempted to push through, but eventually ended the show before the full planned setlist was completed.
